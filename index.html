My Interests

It is a very difficult task to describe things I like and I enjoy, I think once I start those cathegories could be endless. For purpose of this assignment I will discribe a few of those. I have picked what I think are my favorite passtimes.

  • Painting

    • I like this discipline as passtime, doing it and admirign the work of other artists. The vision each human being has on one's suroundings is incredible. It is the reflection of our own selves on a canvas.

  • Traveling

    • One of the hobbies we have as a family is traveling. As incredible as it might seem to some people, it requires a lot of planning, work, and it might be estenous, but the reward is worth all the work. Being able to discover new places, what includes new people, foods, traditions and just the pleasure to open up to new experiences es one of the most rewarding feeling there is.

  • Coding and Mathematics

    • I included these two disciplines together because they have so much in common. The analisys is the drive of both. It is like having a puzzle and finding a way to solve it. Might be a little of a detective inside of me. I could have added solving puzzles as some of the things I enjoy more in life, Investigation, analisys, and solving a task are the food of my inquisitive mind.

I think it is safe to say that I could reduce all the things I like in a few words: discovery, investigate, and solutions. I have an analitical mind. Every day is special to me if that means to learn something new. I think that discovering something new makes each day worth living. To experience life is the most amazing thing a human being has. Enjoying the simple things, and the extraordinary the same way. Life is not perfect, but that imperfection makes it extraordinary. My greatest passion: Life.
