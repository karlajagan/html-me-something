My Interests

My maiden name is Karla Ivonne Sáenz Villagrán and talking about myself and my likes is a very difficult task, as many things bring me joy, I think once I start those categories could be endless. For purpose of this assignment I will describe a few of those. I have picked what I think are my favorite pastimes.

  • Painting

    • I like this discipline as pastime, doing it and admiring the work of other artists. The vision each human being has on one's surroundings is incredible. It is the reflection of our own selves on a canvas.

  • Traveling

    • One of the hobbies we have as a family is traveling. As incredible as it might seem to some people, it requires a lot of planning, work, and it might be strenuous, but the reward is worth all the work. Being able to discover new places, what includes new people, foods, traditions and just the pleasure to open up to new experiences is one of the most rewarding feelings there is.

  • Coding and Mathematics

    • I included these two disciplines together because they have so much in common. The analysis is the drive of both. It is like having a puzzle and finding a way to solve it. Might be a little of a detective inside of me. I could have added solving puzzles, as some of the things I enjoy more in life, Investigation, analysis, and solving a task are the food of my inquisitive mind.

I think it is safe to say that I could reduce all the things I like in a few words: discovery, investigate, and solutions. I have an analytical mind. Every day is special to me if that means to learn something new. I think that discovering something new makes each day worth living. To experience life is the most amazing thing a human being has. Enjoying the simple things, and the extraordinary the same way. Life is not perfect, but that imperfection makes it extraordinary. My greatest passion: Life.
